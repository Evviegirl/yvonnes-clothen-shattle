Our Story

The Beginning

Yvonne's Clothen Shattle was founded in 1985 by Yvonne Montgomery, a visionary fashion designer with a passion for creating timeless office attire that blends sophistication with comfort.

What began as a small boutique in Style City has grown into a respected brand known for its exceptional quality and attention to detail in crafting office pants that stand the test of time.

Yvonne believed that professional attire should never compromise on style or comfort, and that philosophy remains at the heart of everything we create today.

Our Philosophy

At Yvonne's Clothen Shattle, we believe that the perfect pair of pants can transform not just your appearance, but your confidence in the workplace.

Each design is created with meticulous attention to detail, using premium fabrics and traditional tailoring techniques that have been perfected over decades.

We embrace vintage aesthetics while incorporating modern innovations to ensure our garments meet the needs of today's professionals.

Our Core Values

Quality

We never compromise on materials or craftsmanship, ensuring each garment lasts for years.

Sustainability

We are committed to ethical production methods and reducing our environmental footprint.

Attention to Detail

Every stitch, seam, and button is carefully considered for both function and aesthetics.

Community

We proudly support local artisans and maintain close relationships with our customers.
